Recipe

Methods

  1. Chop the chicken into small pieces and marinate with salt and pepper.
  2. Chop the onion, carrot and potato into small pieces.
  3. Heat up the oil in a pan and fry the chicken until golden brown.
  4. Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside.
  5. Heat up the butter in the pan and fry the onion, carrot and potato until soft.
  6. Add the curry and fry for 1 minute.
  7. Add the chicken and fry for 1 minute.
  8. Remove from the heat and serve.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Panko
  • Curry
  • Onion
  • Carrot
  • Potato
  • Butter
  • Oil
